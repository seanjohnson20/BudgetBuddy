Step 1 - Create a Username

To begin click Sign Up and fill out the form. All fields are required and you must accept the terms of use. Click the Sign Up button and you will be returned to the home page.

Step 2 - Add one or more Accounts

Now that you've created a user. You'll need to create at least one Account. I prefer to have this account reconcile to an actual bank account or envelope of money. BudgetBuddy will help you organize how the money in that account is being saved and used. You can create a BudgetBuddy account for every bank account or cash store for which you'd like to track savings. Click the Accounts tab and the Add Accounts button.

Fill out the form and click the Add Account button.

Step 3 - Add one or more Categories

Next you'll need to create some budget categories. These categories are big items that are planned or unplanned expenses that mess up your budget or create debt. Examples include Christmas, car registration or repairs, vacation, or emergency fund.

From the Home page click the Categories tab and the Add Categories button


Fill out the form and click the Add Category button.

Step 4 - Add one or more Goals

This step is optional, but I think you'll find it helpful to motivate you and show you your progress. From the Home page click the Goals tab and the Add Goals button
Select which category relates to this goal. Describe your goal. Enter the target Date and Goal Amount and click the Add Goal button.
Your goal will now appear with details you entered plus how much you'll need to save per month to meet this goal. Below you'll see a progress bar that shows how far you've come and how many days you have left. The bar will change color as you approach your target date and when you meet your goal.

Step 5 - Enter Transactions to track savings

Finally, everytime you add or subtract money from your bank account you should make a like entry here to determine how your savings are being divied up. From the Home page click the Transactions tab and the Add Transactions button

Select from your Accounts and Categories (raquired) and optionally select a Goal. Add notes and Amount and and click the Submit Transaction button.


With each transaction, if you've included a goal, you'll see progress toward your goal.


As more transactions are added and time moves forward you'll have a good picture of how your savings are growing.
